Welcome to my CS50 Final Project - a simple couples' vacation planner

Via the routes master data, plan, overview you can easily manage the annual vacation plan of you and your friend or partner! Please also take a look at the instructions below the photo.

Short Instructions:

The route master data is there for you to tell the site who you are, how many vacation days you have per year and how many days you have left from last year.

The route plan serves the purpose for you to plan your vacations one by one.

After submission, they will be displayed in overview, where you can select the respective year to display all planned vacations. There you will also be able to remove any planned vacations.
